Digital Insecurity

Digital insecurity on social networks is a problem that has been growing quietly but steadily in recent years.

As more people and businesses have migrated to online platforms to interact, share information and conduct transactions, opportunities for cybercriminals have also arisen.

Cyber-attacks, such as phishing, theft of personal data and dissemination of false information, have become common threats in the digital environment of social networks.
