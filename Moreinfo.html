What is a scam?

Scams can take many forms, but they all have a single goal - to take your money.

they get you to reveal personal information, steal it and even make you voluntarily hand over money.

it's important that you can protect yourself from scams so you don't become just another one of the crowd.

we will provide you with sufficient information if you believe you are being scammed or are a victim.

Types of scam

Forms of data theft can vary in so many ways that you don't realize when they knock on your door.
